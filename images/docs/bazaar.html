Bazaar

The Bazaar is where you can buy supplies for your mission. An item will be shown to you. Tap No/End if you wish to move on to the next item. Press Yes/Buy if you wish to purchase the item. You may haggle for a better price by tapping the Haggle button. Be warned: if you haggle too much you will anger the merchant and get nothing!

Warriors are, of course, your troops. You need lots of them if you're going to win the game! You may purchase more than one warrior. To do so tap on Yes/Buy for each warrior you wish to purchase. Tap on No/End when finished.

You need to feed your warriors so you need food! You may purchase more than one ration. To do so tap on Yes/Buy for each ration you wish to purchase. Tap on No/End when finished.

The Beast is a key component to your success. Your warriors can only carry 6 bags of gold each. The Beast can carry 50. Once purchased, the Beast will remain with your for the remainder of the game.

The Scout will keep you from getting lost. Not only that, he will give you an extra turn. Once purchased, the Scout will remain with your for the remainder of the game.

The Healer is amazing! Not only will he prevent you from losing two warriors if you're hit with a plague, he will actually give you two additional ones! Pretty incredible! Once purchased, the Healer will remain with your for the remainder of the game.

The Elf Scout will show you what is in your neighboring territories. Handy for avoiding things you want to avoid! Available in the Enemies & Helpers expansion (see Store).

The Cook knows how to make rations stretch! Your food consumption will be cut in half to a minimum of 1 ration per turn. Available in the Enemies & Helpers expansion (see Store).

There's a chance you may anger the Merchant and he will close up shop! If you haggle too much he will close. Also, if you try to buy something you can't afford he will close. It's important to keep an eye on this when buying warriors or food.
