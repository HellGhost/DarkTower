Combat

What's adventuring without combat? Boring! When you first enter combat you are shown the number of enemies you are facing. Then you will be shown the number of Warriors you have. Then again the number of enemies remaining after the first round. The next round will commence and you will see your Warriors remaining and then the enemies remaining. And so on until you defeat the enemies, they defeat you, or you retreat.

Brigands are the most common enemy you'll face. If you have not purchased the Enemies & Helpers expansion they will be the only ones you encounter. The Brigands numbers drop by half (rounded down) each time they lose a round. Each time you are defeated you lose one warrior.
Goblins will attack you in greater numbers than the Brigands. However, they are are easier to defeat. If they are victorious in a round of battle they will steal something from you! Available only in the Enemies & Helpers expansion (see Store).
Ogres are about as tough as Brigands. However, they're numbers only reduct by 25% when you win a battle round. But if you defeat them you will get a sword! Available only in the Enemies & Helpers expansion (see Store).
Trolls are tough to defeat unless you have a sword. Each round they win you will lose two warriors. Each round you will their numbers will reduce by 25%. If you defeat them you will get Pegasus! Available only in the Enemies & Helpers expansion (see Store).

Victorious!

If you defeat the Brigands you may get something. You will always get gold (unless you're in the Dark Tower or you do not have enough warriors or a beast to carry it). You may also get:

Pegasus You found the magical horse! Pegasus can take you anywhere in your current kingdom or in the next kingdom (providing you have the proper key to travel there). To use Pegaus just tap on it. Then tap on the move icon of the space you wish to move to. If you move into the Frontier you will be able to move into any space in the next kingdom on your next turn. Once you've used Pegaus it flies away.

Dragonsword You found the dragonsword! Now, if the Dragon attacks you, you will defeat it and collect its accumulated gold and warriors!

Wizard You may curse another player and take 25% of that player's warriors and gold! You will be shown a player's number and how many warriors and gold that player has. If you wish to curse that player tap Yes/Buy. If you don't tap No/End. You must always curse a player. When it is the cursed player's turn, that player won't be able to move as he/she recovers from being cursed!

Defeated!

If you are reduced down to one Warrior the battle ends. You can never go below one Warrior UNLESS you are playing a solo game and are attacking the Dark Tower.

Retreating

Sometimes a battle is a little too hard and you need to retreat and regroup. Tap on the No/End button to retreat. You will lose one Warrior for doing this.
